Profile

John Rey Malto is a Filipino Talent Manager. He was born on October 22, 1991 in Manila Philippines. John Rey Malto is now a fulfilled talent manager dedicated to discovering those who have the talent or quality to become a star one day. As he pursues his career, he encourages the younger generation to value education regardless of their social and economic status.One of his goal is to help aspiring artist's to further improve and shape their talents and skills, so that they can fulfill their dreams of becoming an actor, singer, dancer, model or star in the future. This year 2023, John Rey Malto is very grateful because he is one of the recognized Talent Managers in the Showbiz industry today. He added, "if you love your passion, don't give up, just keep going, because if you have perseverance in what your dream of, you will achieve it at the right time."
